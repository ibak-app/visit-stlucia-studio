Taxis

Taxis are plentiful but unmetered â€” always agree on a price before you travel.

Saint Lucia taxis don't have meters. The government has published official rate guidelines, but enforcement is loose. Common routes have well-established "going rates" that most drivers follow, but there's room for negotiation â€” especially for longer journeys or return trips.

Rule 1: Always negotiate before you get in.
Rule 2: Clarify whether the price is in EC$ or US$.
Rule 3: Group travel is good value â€” the same fare per vehicle applies whether there are 1 or 4 passengers.
