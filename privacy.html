Overview

visit.stlucia.studio ("we", "us", or "our") is an independent travel guide to Saint Lucia, operated as part of the stlucia.studio ecosystem. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This is a content-focused guide site ‚Äî we do not sell your data, we do not run targeted advertising, and we aim to collect only the minimum information required to operate the site effectively.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We do not currently have user accounts, contact forms, or email sign-up forms on this site. We collect no personally identifying information directly from visitors through form submission.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, our server logs automatically collect certain technical information when you visit:

  • IP address (anonymised after processing)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring URL (the page that linked you to us)
  • Date and time of visit

Analytics Data

We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Our analytics approach:

  • Does not use advertising cookies
  • Does not track you across other websites
  • Anonymises IP addresses
  • Aggregates data ‚Äî we see trends, not individuals

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Understand which pages and content are most useful to visitors
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our travel information
  • Ensure the website loads correctly across different devices and browsers
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems

We do not use your information for advertising profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose beyond improving this travel guide.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for the site to function correctly (e.g., PWA service worker) Session / Persistent
Analytics Anonymous usage statistics to improve the guide Up to 12 months

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party social media cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

Third-Party Services

We use a small number of third-party services to operate this site:

  • Google Fonts ‚Äî we load the Nunito font from Google's servers. Google may collect anonymised usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting provider ‚Äî our hosting provider (Vercel / GitHub Pages / Cloudflare) processes server logs as part of normal infrastructure operation. These providers have their own privacy policies.

We do not embed third-party advertising, social media widgets, or tracking pixels that would allow third parties to profile you across the web.

External links on this site (to hotels, tour operators, restaurants, government websites) are provided for your convenience. Once you leave our site, their privacy policies apply. We have no control over and accept no responsibility for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

Given the nature of this site (anonymous analytics only, no user accounts, no email collection), we are unlikely to hold any personally identifiable data about individual visitors. However, if you believe we hold data about you and wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
